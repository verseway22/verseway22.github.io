A Visual Development Artist specializing in high-fidelity concept art and early CG visualization for feature animation. With credits spanning Disney, Marvel, Netflix, and 20th Century, Kevin bridges traditional painting with cutting-edge 3D workflows to solve storytelling challenges before they reach the pipeline.

His experimental approach — combining Photoshop, Blender, Houdini, and VR sculpting — creates art-directable environments that give production designers the ability to explore ideas faster and with greater fidelity than traditional methods alone.

Outside the studio, Kevin competes as a professional medieval MMA fighter for the Los Angeles Golden Ring Promotion. Full armor. Real swords. Nickname: "Beyblade."
